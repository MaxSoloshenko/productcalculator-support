# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-20**

ProductCalculator is a private bookkeeping app for home bakers and small bakery owners. This policy explains exactly what happens to the information you put into the app.

## Short version

ProductCalculator does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything you enter lives only on your device.

## What information the app handles

The app lets you record:

- Ingredients, packaging items, and their prices
- Products, recipes, and sale prices
- Sales transactions, including optional customer names
- Customer orders, including names, sources (e.g. Facebook, Instagram), and delivery dates
- Expenses by category
- Business profile fields (business name, owner initials, default note text)

All of this is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's SwiftData framework. The app does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, or any other third-party services.

## What the app does not do

- The app does not create user accounts or require sign-in.
- The app does not connect to the internet for any reason.
- The app does not collect identifiers such as advertising IDs, IDFV, IP address, or device identifiers.
- The app does not track your activity across other apps or websites.
- The app does not share your data with anyone, ever.

## Notifications

If you enable order reminders in Settings, the app will ask iOS for permission to display local notifications. Notification scheduling happens entirely on your device. Apple's notification system does not transmit your reminder content anywhere.

You can revoke notification permission at any time in iOS Settings -> Notifications -> ProductCalculator.

## CSV export and import

The app can export all your data to CSV files saved into your iPhone or iPad's Documents folder. You choose if and where to share those files. Anything that happens to a CSV after export (sharing via Files, Mail, AirDrop, iCloud Drive, etc.) is subject to those services' own privacy practices, not this app's.

## Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone. It is rated 4+ because it has no objectionable content.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app starts collecting any data, this policy will be updated and the change will be called out in the App Store "What's New" section before the new version ships.
